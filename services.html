Company Overview

Ms. Ayush Enkhtaivan is set to found CYBERAWARE CONSULTING LLC in the State of Florida as an LLC Company. This venture does not require a high investment to implement. Start-up costs will be covered by personal savings, while subsequent years will be self-funded through revenue generated from consulting services.

Market Focus

The demand analysis for cybersecurity consulting services in the U.S. reveals a robust market opportunity. CYBERAWARE CONSULTING LLC will initially target companies broadly, with plans to expand by opening branches in strategic locations to better serve clients across sectors.

Vision and Commitment

CyberAware Consulting LLC is a forward-thinking cybersecurity firm established in Florida’s vibrant technology ecosystem. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to navigate the digital world with confidence and resilience.

Through our services, we aim to secure a digitally safer future for our clients and their stakeholders.
